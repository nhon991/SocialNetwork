Frequently Asked Questions
Account
How do i create a Banana account?

To create a Banana account:

  1. 1. Go to Login page
  2. 2. Click Sign up
  3. 3. Enter your name, date of birth, email, password and gender
  4. 4. Click Create account
  5. 5. To finish creating your account, you need to confirm your email
How can I make my password strong?

When you create a password, keep in mind:

  • * Your password should be easy for you to remember but difficult for others to guess.
  • * Your Banana password should be different than the passwords you use to log into other accounts, like your email or bank account.
  • * Longer passwords are usually more secure.
  • * Your password should not be your email, phone number or birthday.

How do I find and add friends on Banana?

To add a friend on Banana:

  1. 1. Tap Search in the top left
  2. 2. Enter your friend's name
  3. 3. Select their name
  4. 4. To send someone a friend request, tap Add friend next to their name
How do I unfriend or remove a friend?

To unfriend someone:

  1. 1. Go to that person's profile
  2. 2. Hover over Friends on the top of their profile
  3. 3. Select Unfriend

The person you unfriended won't be notified.

Your Home Page

Your home page is what you see when you log into Banana. It includes your News Feed, the constantly updating list of posts from friends, Pages and other connections you've made. You can react to things you see or search for people and topics that matter to you.

What kinds of posts will I see in New Feeds?

Posts that you see in News Feed are meant to keep you connected to the people, places and things that you care about, starting with your friends and family. Posts that you see first are influenced by your connections and activity on Banana. The number of comments, likes and reactions a post receives and what kind of story it is (example: photo, video, status update) can also make it more likely to appear higher up in your News Feed. Posts that you might see first include:

  • * A friend or family member commenting on or liking another friend's photo or status update.
  • * A person reacting to a post from a publisher that a friend has shared.
  • * Multiple people replying to each otherâ€™s comments on a video they watched or an article they read in News Feed.
How do I follow or unfollow a profile or a Page?

To follow a profile or a Page:

  1. 1. Go to the profile or Page
  2. 2. Click follow

To unfollow a profile or a Page:

  1. 1. Go to the profile or Page
  2. 2. Hover over Following
  3. 3. Select Unfollow
What does it mean to "Like" something?

Clicking Like below a post on Banana is a way to let people know that you enjoy it without leaving a comment. Just like a comment, anyone who can see the post can see that you liked it.

For example, if you click Like below a friend's video:

  • * People who can see the video will be able to see that you liked it.
  • * The person who posted the video will get a notification that you liked it.
How do I unlike something?

You can only unlike posts, photos, comments that you previously liked. To unlike them:

  1. 1. Go to the post, photo or comments.
  2. 2. Click Like to Unlike
How do I see who's reacted to my post or comment?

To see who's reacted to your post or comment, click the icons directly below your post or comment, and then select an option at the top to see who chose that reaction.

What can I search for on Banana?

Banana search results are based on your Banana activity. Banana search results aren't influenced by activity off Banana.

You can search for people, dishes and restaurants. Start searching with Keywords and you'll see a list of results.

Messaging

Messaging on Banana lets you instantly reach people you care about. You can send your friends photos, attachments, stickers and GIFs from Banana chat on your computer.

How do I send a message on Banana?

To send a direct message on Banana on a computer:

  1. 1. Click Mesages on the top right of the screen.
  2. 2. Click New Message.
  3. 3. Start typing a name into the To field. Names of friends will appear in a dropdown.
  4. 4. Select the person or people you want to message.
  5. 5. Type your message, then press enter to send.

You can also message someone by clicking Message at the top of their profile.

Who can see my Banana massage?

Only you and the people youâ€™re messaging can view your conversation. Each of your conversations is separate. Even if some of the same people are in more than one conversation, we don't combine those conversations into one thread. For example, if you have conversation with just Jane, and then Jane is included in a different conversation among a larger group of friends, the original thread will stay separate from the group conversation.

Photos and Videos

You can share one photo or videp from restaurants you've been to, or dishes you've had. It's up to you to choose who can see your photos.

How do I share photos or videos on Banana?

To share photos and videos on Banana:

  1. 1. At the top of your News Feed or timeline, click Photo/Video below Create Post.
  2. 2. Select the photos or videos you want to share
  3. 3. Select your audience, then click Share.
Login and Password

If you know your current password, you can change it.

How do I log into my Facebook account?

To log into your Banana account:

  1. 1. Go to Login Page.
  2. 2. Under Username, enter your username.
  3. 3. Under Password, enter your password.
  4. 4. Click Log in.
How do I log out of Banana?

To log out of Banana:

  1. 1. Click at the top right of any Banana page.
  2. 2. Click Log Out at the bottom of the menu that appears.
How do I change my Banana password?

To change your password on Banana if you're already logged in:

  1. 1. Click in the top right corner of any Banana page and select Account setting.
  2. 2. Click Change password.
  3. 3. Enter your current password and new password.
  4. 4. Click Save Changes.
How do I reset my Banana password?

To reset your password if you're not logged in to Banana:

  1. 1. Go to the Forgotten password Page.
  2. 2. Type the email, mobile phone number, full name or username associated with your account, then click Search.
  3. 3. Follow the on-screen instructions.
Your Profile and Settings

Your profile tells your story. You can choose what to share, such as the dishes you like most, the restaurant you usually come, and who to share it with.

How do I add or edit the public information that's shown on my Banana profile?

You can see the public information that's shown on your profile( such as name, hometown) in the Intro section below your profile picture.

To add or edit the public info that's shown on your profile:

  1. 1. From your News Feed, tap your name in the top left.
  2. 2. Hover over the Intro section below your profile picture and click Edit.
  3. 3. Add or change the info you'd like to show in your Intro section and click Save.
